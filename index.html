ABOUT US

Get ready to rock with KöTTBULLAR, the indie band hailing from Munich!


Our catchy and energetic tunes exude punk and power-pop vibes that have won the hearts of fans all around the world.
KöTTBULLAR has received tremendous support from indie radio stations globally and is recognized as one of the most exciting and promising indie rock bands out there.
Join the KöTTBULLAR experience and let our music lift your spirits and inspire bravery. Don't miss out on this electrifying band that's determined to leave a lasting impression on the music world.
